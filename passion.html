Passion

  • Hobbies:Cooking, Travelling

Travelling : I enjoy travelling and exploring different places around the world. Having travelled to over 10 countries in the past 3 years, I have grown to appreciate different culture, the way of life and their food.

Cooking : My passion for cooking stems from my best memories as a child, cooking and eating soul soothing food made by my Mom and Aaji(Grandmom). I share recipes from Vidarbha region, the simple delicion homemade food that I leart to cook. The aim with the channel is to let more and more people know about Marathi food and carry on the culture thorugh the genration.
