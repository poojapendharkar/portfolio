WEBSITE DESIGN - INTRODUCING NEW SERVICES AND PRODUCT

  • Project:Landing Page design
  • Industry:Ecommerce - Furniture

New Service: An ecommerce furniture brand wanted to introduce new feature for custom design solutions. The feature was to be incorporated and plugged onto the new site ensuring that the design matches the overall theme and yet stands out to showcase the unique new aspect of the business. Considering the client's requirement I led the delivery of the landing page design along with content writing and final delivery to make this feature go live.

New Collection: Brand new collections were introduced in the workplace furniture solutions range. The three new collections had to be showcased through intuitive landing page design to new customers. The aim was also to share new collection with existing customers to improve enagement and drive sales. The three pages in collections tab were designed and content was accordingly created with quick delivery.

WEBSITE REDESIGN - STAY RELEVANT, SEARCHABLE AND ATTRACT LEADS

  • Project:Website Redesign
  • Industry:B2B

B2B SaaS Site Design: The startup struggled to attract new clients due to unclear messaging and complex design. The user journey was mapped to design the website flow and craft strong messaging.

Workspace solutions landing page: The client wanted to attract new customers for a business segment that offered TAAS (Technology as a service). The landing page was designed along with other marketing efforts to drive traffic, and convert leads.
